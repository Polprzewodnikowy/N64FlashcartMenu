## N64 Information

![Example N64 system information](./images/system-information.png "Example N64 system information")  
This screen will show information about your N64 console. At the time of writing, it will show the status of the Expansion PAK, and all the Controller ports (and accessories).

Detection a physical Disk Drive will always be shown as "Unknown". Full support for detection will be added at a later date.
